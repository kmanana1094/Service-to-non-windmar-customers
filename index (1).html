Service to Non-Windmar Customers — Standard Operating Procedure
Guidelines for servicing solar systems not originally installed by Windmar Home
SOP Document
This SOP governs all service lead handling for customers whose solar and/or battery systems were not originally installed by Windmar Home. Follow the channel-specific entry procedure, then route through SAP for lead creation and closer assignment.
Lead Inquiry Channels / Canales de Entrada de Lead
Tesla Page
  • Customer inquires themselves via the Tesla Page
  • Email: sap@windmarhome.com
  • Customer contact info and service request may not be included
  • We must respond to their emails immediately
Rep Generated
  • Sales Rep creates the lead and marks product as "Service"
  • Sales Rep sends request to SAP to create and book the customer
Customer Call-In (Inbound)
  • Customer calls in to inquire for service
  • Customer will be transferred to SAP
  • SAP will create lead in Zoho and follow SOP process for lead creation & assignment
Lead Entry & Assignment Process / Proceso de Entrada y Asignación del Lead
1
Lead Entry
  • Customer Call-In (Inbound)
  • Sales Rep Entered
  • Sales Rep Requests Lead Entry
2
SAP Collects Customer Info
  • Contacts customer to confirm contact information
  • Confirms service inquired
  • Marks service type in Zoho manually until Command is accessible
  • Creates proposal in Command or manually in Zoho
3
SAP Assigns Closer
  • Verifies Closer Distribution — assigns in Rotation
  • Ilham Safazada
  • Abdiel Mendez
  • Aby Gonzalez
  • Juan Sebastian Munoz
  • SAP provides County property appraiser details; alerts Rep of lead source & product
4
Dispositions
  • Subcategory will be accessible for Service Leads
  • In the meantime: update dispositions as standard; notate next steps in lead notes
  • Service tech assigned after SALE is completed; will update service ticket
  • Must report back to rep once service is completed
All services below are available to non-Windmar customers at published rates. Review included scope and warranty terms carefully with the customer before scheduling.
Available Services / Servicios Disponibles
EV Charger Installation
Instalación de Cargador EV
  • Furnish and install Level 2 Tesla Wall Connector
  • Conduit and wiring up to 50 ft (charger to Panelboard/Gateway)
  • Programming and set-up
  • In-person customer training (same day)
  • Professional Service Report
WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY: 1 YEAR
PV System Removal
Remoción de Sistema FV
  • Remove system components: modules, rails, conduit, wires
  • System attachments (penetrations) remain unless customer arranged sealing
  • Material organized on site in coordination with customer
  • Professional Service Report
NO WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY
PV System Re-installation
Reinstalación de Sistema FV
  • Re-installation of existing solar components: modules, rails, conduit, wires
  • Same original system layout will be maintained
  • Complete system connections (AC/DC)
  • System commissioning and start-up
  • Monitoring app set-up and in-person training (same day)
  • Professional Service Report
WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY: 30 DAYS
Design / Permit / Inspection
Diseño / Permiso / Inspección
  • Full system engineering design
  • Permit application submission
  • Processing, coordinate and perform system inspection
  • AHJ-required changes evaluated separately; additional charges may apply
NO WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY
Module Cleaning
Limpieza de Módulos
  • Professional solar module cleaning
  • Professional Service Report
NO WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY
Main Service Panel (MSP) Upgrade
Actualización de Panel Principal
  • Site Survey (Pre-Inspection Visit)
  • Engineering Design
  • Permit application submission
  • Processing, coordinate and perform system inspection
  • Coordinate with Utility Company for disconnect & reconnect
  • Furnish and install new MSP (1 ea) — outdoor installation
  • Install conduit and wire from meter base to MSP
  • Not included: interior panelboard replacement, circuit loads, or fixing existing circuits
WORKMANSHIP WARRANTY: 1 YEAR
System Troubleshooting / Diagnostics
Diagnóstico y Solución de Averías
  • Service Visit (1 ea.)
  • System evaluation, diagnostic & verification of all electrical connections
  • Replace damaged solar system fuses and/or breakers
  • Restore system communication (Internet connection)
  • Set-up monitoring app with the customer
  • Technical support assistance with equipment/component manufacturers
  • Handling warranty claims with manufacturers (RMA process)
  • Not included: equipment replacement/warranty claims, damaged conduit/wires, roofing, seal penetrations, remove/reinstall systems, additional visits
WARRANTY: 30 DAYS (WITHIN SCOPE PERFORMED ONLY)
Florida residential services only. Equipment subject to manufacturer warranty.
Important Notes / Notas Importantes
  • Windmar Home does not warranty equipment or workmanship of third-party installers.
  • All services require a signed work order and payment agreement prior to scheduling.
  • Roof penetration sealing is NOT included in removal services — customer must coordinate separately.
  • Technician may decline work if safety hazards or equipment condition prevents safe completion.
Once a service lead is marked SOLD, the following cycle is initiated. NTP reviews the account, a service tech is scheduled, and the lead is closed in the pipeline upon job completion.
Post-Sale Service Cycle / Ciclo de Servicio Post-Venta
01
Service Lead — SOLD
Closer marks the lead as SOLD in Zoho. Sale is recorded against the rep's account.
02
Lead Converted by Accounting
Accounting team converts the lead and processes payment collection confirmation.
03
Enters NEW Service Pipeline
The converted lead enters the dedicated Service Pipeline for scheduling and tracking.
04
NTP Reviews Smartsheet & Docs
NTP verifies Smartsheet and required documents. Confirms payment was collected by Sales Rep before proceeding.
05
Service Tech is Scheduled *BY WHO???*
Service technician is assigned and scheduled for on-site visit. Assignment owner pending process clarification.
06
Service Completes Job
Tech completes the job on site. If additional work is required beyond original quote, payment is collected on site.
07
Service Complete — Lead Marked End of Stage / Pipeline
Service is marked complete. Rep is notified. Professional Service Report is finalized and delivered to the customer.
